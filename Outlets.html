Family Feast

A restaurant serving a multitude of food items
from your favourite fast food venues, from
Marios Pizza to Mc Donalds. Located in the
adult rides section near the Shockwave ride.

Long John Silvers

Come dine on the well prepared meals of this well
known nautical themed restaurant. Located in
the kiddy rides section, opposite the Pirates
Cove ride, try the fish and chips special!.

Mr.Frosty's Iced Treats

Here we have a variety of cold treats including
icecream, snow cones and even special treats from
Dairy Queen and Sonic. Located in the kiddy
rides section, next to the Mr. Frosty ride.

Tiki Cafe

This is quiet place to relax and unwind, serving
primarily non-alcoholic caffeinated drinks. We
trust you'll need the energy for what's ahead. Find
it in the adult rides section near the Volcano ride.

Bar Locations

There are two bars here at the park that serve
a variety of non-alcoholic and your favourite
alcoholic drinks. Located in the adults rides section,
Find them near the Lovers Lane and Volcano rides.
