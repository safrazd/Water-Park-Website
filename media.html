Welcome to the MEDIA Page
This page describes all of the events that are hosted by the Water Park and events that you can host. To be part of our events or host your own events, please go to the contact us page and make your request. As well view our photos taken of you at the Water Park.

Birthday Bash with a Splash

Want to have the most exciting and fantastic Brithday ever!!!
Well guess what?! The UWI Water Park accommodates for your Birthday! Just tell us what day, time and your favourite theme.

And yes, we do have our own catering service: Chinese, Indian, Creole, Italian and a wide range of desserts inclusive of Birthday Cakes.

Extra Information: For queries about anything that is not listed please contact us.

Christmas with a Splash

Feeling for a getaway for Christmas? Want to make this Christmas a family oriented one?
Our cabins will be fully decorated to give you that Christmas Feel as well as that warm, homey feeling.

There will be many activities to help you and family bond. These include:

Kids Treat-an evening of activities dedicated to Kids, Mingle-for adults, supervision will be provided for kids Dining- complimentary Christmas breakfast and special dinners, Indoor Skiing- artificial snow will be provided

Extra Information - For queries about anything that is not listed please contact us

Other Splashin' Events

These events can be hosted by UWI Water Park or You.

Yes, you can contact the water park to book it as a venue to host whatever event you are having: a reception, family gathering, wedding etc.
We will make the transformation for you!
The water park also hosts its own events from time to time, so do look out for any notices.
