We care about the environment here at UWI Water Park

Environmentally friendly technology at UWI Water Park

Here at UWI Water Park all of our water systems employ non-evasive technologies such as carbon treatment and micron filtration. These technologies help produce water that is 100% clean and is without any chemical residues. We also generate 75% of our energy by using solar panels located throughout the water park car parks.

Water Filtration

At the park, water is cleaned using moss filtration devices. This allows water to be recycled back into the park which saves us hundreds of thousands of gallons of water each year.

Recycling

Recycling bins are located all around the park. At UWI Water Park we make every effort to keep the surroundings clean.
